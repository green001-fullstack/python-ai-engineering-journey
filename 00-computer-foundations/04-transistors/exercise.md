# Question 3
### Why is binary such a natural choice for computers?

Because binary just like transistors represents two states.

# Question 4
### Why can't a single transistor perform complex tasks like addition or multiplication?

A single transistor is only an electronic switch. Complex tasks like addition or multiplication require many transistors working together in carefully designed circuits.

# Question 5 (The Most Important)
### Explain this sentence in your own words:

"Everything in modern computing is built on billions of tiny switches."

Every program, website, game, and AI model ultimately depends on billions of transistors switching on and off. Those tiny switches are combined to form larger components like logic gates, registers, and CPUs, which eventually execute our software.
